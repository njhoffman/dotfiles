About Us

SpaDash, Inc. was founded in Austin, Texas. It has one primary focus - to provide a great haircut to our customers at any place and at any time. People are busy these days and expect to get exceptional service on their own time. SpaDash provides a haircutting service that allows busy people make the best of their time, and less time wasted on standing in line and driving.
